linux:


$ wget http://stedolan.github.io/jq/download/linux32/jq (32-bit system)
$ wget http://stedolan.github.io/jq/download/linux64/jq (64-bit system)
$ chmod +x ./jq
$ sudo cp jq /usr/bin
